Boarding at Parivarthana

A healthy child requires not only a loving home but also a supportive school to grow into a confident individual. At Parivarthana, our boarding facilities are designed with this in mind, offering spacious dormitories, a dedicated house parent for each dormitory, and healthy, delicious meals that nurture both body and spirit. The hostel is supported by the cafeteria, a fitness centre and a 24 hour medical centre. Experienced House Parents, tutors and service staff take care of children and their individual needs.

Library

With a wide collection of books, journals, and digital resources, students have access to a wealth of information that supports their academic growth. The library offers a peaceful environment where students can focus on their studies, explore new interests, or enjoy recreational reading. Our library is not just a place for books, but a hub for creativity, research, and intellectual curiosity.

Transportation

Our transportation services include a fleet of well-maintained buses that are available to take students on trips, tours, and other educational or recreational events. These buses are equipped with safety features and driven by experienced staff to ensure a comfortable and secure journey.
